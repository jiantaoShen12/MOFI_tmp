Downstream Analysis

Inter-Omics Dynamical Mechanism Exploration.

MOFI enables a suite of downstream analyses that go beyond trajectory inference — from predicting how perturbing one omics layer propagates to others, to discovering cross-omics regulatory programs and reconstructing developmental lineages.

Cross-Omics Perturbation Response

Perturbing one omics and predicting the cascading response across the other omics, revealing real dynamic coupling beyond simple correlation.

Cross-Omics Regulatory Programs

Clustering genes by their regulatory impact on the target omics, uncovering functional groups with similar cross-omics influence patterns.

Multi-Omics Stage Dynamics

Quantifying temporal program scores across early, transition, and late stages, capturing the stage-specific activation of multi-omics developmental programs.

Multi-Omics Lineage Reconstruction

Sankey visualization of cell-type fate transitions across developmental stages, including prediction of unseen time points and ancestral lineage origins.
